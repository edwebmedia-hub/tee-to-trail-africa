More Than Just Golf

Built on a passion for courses, culture, and connection — this is who we are.

10+
Years on the Fairways

Werner Loubser

After years of exploring golf courses and hiking trails across the globe, Werner recognised what most golf tours were missing — authentic local experience, genuine personalisation, and adventures that extend beyond the 18th hole.

"I started Tee to Trail Africa because I wanted other golfers to experience what I'd spent years discovering — that the best trips aren't just about the course. They're about the whole journey."

Based in Wellington, South Africa, Werner and the Tee to Trail team draw on deep local relationships and firsthand knowledge of every course, region, and hidden gem we recommend. When you book with us, you're not getting a generic package — you're getting the benefit of someone who has actually played those courses, stayed in those lodges, and knows exactly what makes each destination special.
