International Golf

Take your game beyond South Africa — championship courses across Asia, Africa, and beyond.

Asia Africa

Asia

Championship golf across some of the world's most extraordinary landscapes — coastal fairways, highland greens, and rice terrace backdrops across three remarkable countries.

3 Destinations Vietnam · Sri Lanka · Indonesia
Asia — Vietnam

Vietnam

One of Asia's most captivating golf destinations — championship courses from Hanoi's tranquil lakeside fairways to Da Nang's seaside layouts, Dalat's highland greens, and Ho Chi Minh City's modern courses. Between rounds, explore Ha Long Bay, Sapa's rice terraces, and the ancient streets of Hoi An.

Hanoi — Lakeside Da Nang — Coastal Dalat — Highland Ha Long Bay Cultural Immersion
Enquire About Vietnam
Asia — Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka

The "Pearl of the Indian Ocean" — world-class golf paired with extraordinary natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Play Victoria Golf & Country Resort, the historic Royal Colombo Golf Club, and coastal fairways at Hambantota. Then explore tea-covered hills, ancient temples, and wildlife safaris.

Victoria Golf Resort Royal Colombo GC Wildlife Safaris Tea Hill Country Ancient Temples
Enquire About Sri Lanka
Asia — Indonesia

Indonesia

A golfer's paradise across thousands of islands — Bali's coastal fairways with ocean views, Jakarta's modern city courses, and Java's highland greens amid volcanoes and rice terraces. Extend your trip to the temples of Borobudur, the Komodo Islands, or Sumatra's ancient rainforests.

Bali — Coastal Jakarta — City Java — Highland Volcano Backdrops Komodo Islands
Enquire About Indonesia

Africa

Epic golf in some of the continent's most dramatic settings — from Namibia's desert dune courses to the Victoria Falls region, where every round comes with a wildlife encounter.

2 Destinations Namibia · Zimbabwe, Zambia & Botswana
Africa — Namibia

Namibia

Golf unlike anywhere else on earth — dramatic desert dune backdrops, courses along the haunting Skeleton Coast, and Windhoek's well-maintained city layouts. Beyond the fairways: Etosha National Park, wild Atlantic coast fishing, and the ancient dunes of the Namib Desert — the oldest in the world.

Desert Dune Settings Skeleton Coast Windhoek Courses Etosha NP Namib Desert
Enquire About Namibia
Africa — Zimbabwe, Zambia & Botswana

Zimbabwe, Zambia & Botswana

Southern Africa's most epic triple-destination experience — the Victoria Falls region fairways, Phakalane and Kasane in Botswana, and Zambian greens along the Zambezi. Sunset over the falls between rounds, Chobe River cruises for elephant and hippo encounters, and the breathtaking Okavango Delta.

Victoria Falls Phakalane — Botswana Zambezi Sunsets Chobe Wildlife Okavango Delta
